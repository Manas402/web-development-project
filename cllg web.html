Welcome to Amal Jyothi College of Engineering

About Us

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE) is one of Kerala's leading engineering colleges, known for its innovation, academic excellence, and industry collaboration. Established in 2001, it is the first engineering college in Kerala accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade.

Departments

  • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS)
  • Civil Engineering (CE)
  • Food Technology

Library

The college has a **state-of-the-art digital library** with thousands of books, e-journals, and research materials.

Facilities

  • Advanced Computing Labs with High-Performance Servers
  • Fully Equipped Mechanical and Electronics Labs
  • Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Center (IEDC)
  • On-Campus Hostel and Cafeteria
  • Wi-Fi Enabled Smart Classrooms

Faculty

AJCE has a team of highly experienced faculty members, many of whom hold PhDs from top institutions and have extensive industry experience.

Placements

The college has **strong industry partnerships** and offers excellent placement opportunities in companies like **Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Bosch, Amazon, and Microsoft**.
